About

Arizona Multihull Fleet 42 is an Arizona beach catamaran
sailing club. We're an open fleet, home to all makes and sizes of
beach catamarans. From A-Cats to Tornados and all the cats in between
you are welcome in our fleet!

G-Cats, Formula16/18, Hobies, Mysteres, Nacras, Sol Cats, and SuperCats
(to name a few) are some of the classic catamarans and modern high-speed
spinnaker catamarans you will find in our fleet.

You can contact club members and learn details about events at Fleet 42 Facebook page
