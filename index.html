About

  Arizona Multihull Fleet 42 is an Arizona beach catamaran sailing club.
  We're an open fleet, home to all makes of beach catamarans.  A-Cats, 
  G-Cats, Formula 16/18, Hobies, Mysteres, Nacras, Sol Cats, SuperCats 
  and Tornados are some of the classic catamarans and modern high-speed 
  spinnaker catamarans you will find in our fleet.
